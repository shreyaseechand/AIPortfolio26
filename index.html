About me

From the Himalayas to California.

Growing up in the shadows of the Himalayas taught me a fundamental lesson: the only way is up. My passion for learning, leading, and dreaming big brought me to the USA to pursue my master's at Indiana University Bloomington. From there, I moved to Silicon Valley in 2013 and have ever since worked alongside distinguished design teams across diverse industries, building shared visions through roles at Intuit, Expedia, ChargePoint, and Chewy.

I believe design starts with curiosity and thrives on collaboration. I love bringing this to life by facilitating workshops that cut through ambiguity, visualizing complexity, and building solutions that are robust, feasible, and impactful.

When I am not designing, you'll often find me exploring new destinations, diving into different cultures, and discovering local flavors. I channel my creativity through movement and making—whether I'm sketching, painting, dancing, or getting hands-on in craft workshops.
